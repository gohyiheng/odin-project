Teriyaki Chicken

Description

Teriyaki chicken is a japanese dish that consists of chicken which is marinated in teriyaki sauce. The sauce consists of ingredients such as soy sauce, mirin and sugar. This gives the chicken a sweet and savoury taste.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • 2Tbsp Mirin
  • 1Tbsp Soy sauce
  • 1-2Tbsp Sugar

Directions

  1. Remove excess fat from the chicken, poke several holes into the chicken with a fork.
  2. Heat a small amount of oil in a frying pan over medium heat.
  3. Place chicken skin side down and sear for 3-4 minutes or till golden brown.
  4. Flip the chicken and cover the pan with a lid, let it simmer for about 4 minutes.
  5. Remove the lid and wipe out the excess oil with a paper towel.
  6. Add the mirin, soy sauce and sugar and let it simmer at a medium heat.
  7. Once the sauce thickens, saute both sides of the chicken and you're done!
