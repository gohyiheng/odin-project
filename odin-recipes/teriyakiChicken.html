Teriyaki Chicken

Description

Teriyaki chicken is a japanese dish that consists of chicken which is marinated in teriyaki sauce. The sauce consists of ingredients such as soy sauce, mirin and sugar. This gives the chicken a sweet and savoury taste.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • 2Tbsp Mirin
  • 1Tbsp Soy sauce
  • 1-2Tbsp Sugar
